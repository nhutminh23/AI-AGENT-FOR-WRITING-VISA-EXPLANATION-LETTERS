APPENDIX 5

SALE PRICE AND PAYMENT SCHEDULE

(Attached to the Sale and Purchase Agreement for houses/construction works No. NWP.5.5-2.28/2024/HDMB/DVBT-TNP-NLKN dated 10 June 2024)

1. The Sale Price of the Resort Property is 11.906.428.652 VND (In words: Eleven billion nine hundred six million four hundred twenty-eight thousand six hundred fifty-two dong only), of which:

(a) The Sale Price of the Resort Property inclusive of land lease and exclusive of value added tax is 10.824.026.047 VND (In words: Ten billion eight hundred twenty-four million twenty-six thousand forty-seven dong only);

(b) The value added tax of the Resort Property is 1.082.402.605 VND (In words: One billion eighty-two million four hundred two thousand six hundred five dong only). For clarification, value added tax is calculated directly on the Sale Price of the Resort Property exclusive of value added tax at the tax rate provided by law at the time of signing this Agreement (hereinafter referred to as VAT).

2. The Sale Price of the Resort Property may be adjusted upon the occurrence of at least one of the following cases:

(a) The Parties agree that the Total Usable Floor Area may differ (increase or decrease) in reality based on measurement on the Handover Date. If the actual Total Usable Floor Area differs within 2% (two percent) compared to the Total Usable Floor Area stipulated in Appendix 1 of this Agreement, the Parties shall not adjust the Sale Price of the Resort Property. If the actual Total Usable Floor Area differs by more than 2% (two percent) compared to the Total Usable Floor Area stipulated in Appendix 1 of this Agreement, then the Sale Price of the Resort Property exclusive of VAT shall be adjusted upward (+) or downward (-) correspondingly by the difference in total usable floor area multiplied (x) by 12.400.000 VND/m2.

(b) The Parties agree that the Total Land Use Area may differ (increase or decrease) in reality based on measurement on the Handover Date. If the actual Total Land Use Area differs within 2% (two percent) compared to the Total Land Use Area stipulated in Article 1 of this Agreement, the Parties shall not adjust the Sale Price of the Resort Property. If the actual Total Land Use Area differs by more than 2% (two percent) compared to the Total Land Use Area stipulated in Article 1 of this Agreement, then the Sale Price of the Resort Property exclusive of VAT shall be adjusted upward (+) or downward (-) correspondingly by the difference in total land use area multiplied (x) by 37.577.186 VND/m2.

(c) In the event the VAT changes due to changes in the VAT rate under the law from the effective date of this Agreement until the handover date of the Property, the Parties shall execute an appendix to adjust the Sale Price of the Resort Property accordingly.

(d) Payment of the value of the difference (if any) resulting from (i) the increase/decrease of the Total Land Use Area and/or the Total Usable Floor Area and/or (ii) the change in the VAT rate under Item 2 of this Appendix shall be made by the Buyer pursuant to the Seller’s notice.

3. For avoidance of doubt, for the purposes of Items 2(a) and 2(b) above, the Total Usable Floor Area and Total Land Use Area difference shall mean the portion of area actually exceeding 2% (two percent) compared to the Total Usable Floor Area or Total Land Use Area.

When comparing the Total Usable Floor Area and the Total Land Use Area, the Parties must use the same measurement method. The actual Total Usable Floor Area and the actual Total Land Use Area are the areas mutually agreed by the Parties and recorded in the Handover Minutes.
