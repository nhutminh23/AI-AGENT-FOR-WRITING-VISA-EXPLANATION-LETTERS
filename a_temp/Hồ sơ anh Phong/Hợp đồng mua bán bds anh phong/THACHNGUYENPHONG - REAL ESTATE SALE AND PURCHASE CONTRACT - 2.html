ANNEX 1

DEFINITION OF TERMS

(Attached to the Sale and Purchase Agreement for house/construction works No. NWP.5.5-
2.28/2024/HDMB/DVBT-TNP-NLKN dated 10 January 2024)

1. Unless the context otherwise requires, the capitalized terms below in this Agreement have
the following meanings:

"Resort Property" : means including resort villas, shophouses and other resort properties
belonging to the Project constructed by the Investor for sale/long-term lease, details as
stipulated in Article 1 of this Agreement.

"Handover Record" : means the minutes prepared on the Handover Date for the purpose
of handing over the Resort Property. The Handover Record shall record the Total Usable
Floor Area and the Total Land Usable Area actually used to calculate the Sale Price of the
Resort Property and to confirm the condition, state, and record other necessary contents
related to the handover of the Resort Property.

"Project" : means the real estate project of the Investor named To Hop Khu du lich Thung
Lung Dai Duong located at Tien Thanh Commune, Phan Thiet City.

"Resort Property Sale Price" : means the total purchase price for each Resort Property as provided in Article 2 and Annex 5 attached to this Agreement.

"Certificate" : means the Certificate of land use rights, ownership of residential houses and
other assets attached to the land for the Resort Property issued to the Purchaser by the
competent State authority in accordance with the law.

"Agreement" : means this Sale and Purchase Agreement for the Resort Property and all
appendices attached thereto as well as any written amendments and supplements to this
Agreement made and signed by the Parties in the course of performing this Agreement.

"Resort Property Area" : means the area comprising the Resort Properties of the Project.

"Common Area" : means the common area of the Resort Property Area and the common
area of the Project as stipulated in Annex 4 of this Agreement which the Purchaser/occupants
of the Resort Property and the Seller shall use in common in accordance with this Agreement.
"Private Area" : means the area of the Project that is the private property of the Purchaser
under this Agreement and/or the private property of the Seller, as the context may require.

"Operation Management Fee" : means the fee that the owner or any person using the Resort Property must pay for the management and operation services of the Resort Property Area as stipulated in Annex 6 attached to this Agreement, including but not limited to related costs to ensure the normal operation and/or functioning of the Resort Property Area as prescribed and applied by the Investor appropriately depending on the time and the actual operation of the Resort Property Area.

"Handover Date" : means the date on which the Resort Property is actually handed over to the
Purchaser as stipulated in Article 5 of this Agreement.

"Estimated Handover Date" : means the date on which the Seller is expected to hand over the Resort Property to the Purchaser as provided in Article 5 of this Agreement.

"Working Day" : means a calendar day excluding Saturdays, Sundays, and public holidays
and Tet holidays as prescribed by the law of Vietnam.

"Resort Property Rules" : means all rules and regulations that the Purchaser and/or occupants
in the Resort Property Area must comply with and observe relating to the use of the Resort
Property, the Common Area, and utility works during the management and use of the Resort
Property issued by the Seller, the management and operation unit of the Resort Property Area
or any party designated/authorized by the Seller.

"Force Majeure Event" : means an event that occurs objectively which any Party to this
Agreement could not foresee and could not prevent even after having applied all necessary
and reasonably practicable measures. Cases considered force majeure events are provided
for in Article 13 of this Agreement.
"Credit Institution" : means a credit institution lawfully established and operating in Vietnam
that receives the collateral of assets/rights under the Agreement from the Purchaser.

"Total Gross Floor Area" : means the total floor area of all levels, including basements,
semi-basements, mezzanines, technical floors, attics and stair towers. The floor area of a
floor is the gross floor area of that floor, including enclosing walls (or the portion of common
wall belonging to the house) and the footprint area of loggias, balconies, staircases, elevator
shafts, technical shafts and flues.

The Total Gross Floor Area stipulated in Article 1.3.(a) of the Agreement is a provisional
area and will be determined precisely when actual measurement is carried out on the
Handover Date, as recorded in the Handover Record.

"Total Usable Floor Area" : Total usable floor area of the Resort Property: 175,00 m2, is the total
usable floor plate area of the floors (excluding roof level) of the Resort Property measured in
square metres (m2) and rounded to one decimal place. Accordingly, the Total Usable Floor
Area includes the area measured from the outer face of enclosing walls of all floors (except
the roof level), the ground-level porch area with structural columns and roof covering, the
area of technical shafts, roof/covered drying terrace area, balcony area. The Total Usable
Floor Area in this clause is provisional and shall be determined precisely when actual
measurement is carried out on the Handover Date, as recorded in the Handover Record. For
the avoidance of doubt, the Total Usable Floor Area does not include the area of ancillary
works such as swimming pool area, garden area.

"Total Land Usable Area" : means the entire land area within the boundary of the resort property measured in square metres (m2) and rounded to one decimal place. The Total Land Usable Area in this clause is provisional and will be determined precisely when actual measurement is carried out on the Handover Date, as recorded in the Handover Record.

The Total Land Usable Area as stipulated in Article 1 attached to this Agreement.

(ii) words denoting a gender shall include all genders;
(iii) words such as "other", "including" and "in particular" do not limit the generality of any
word preceding them and shall not be construed as limited to the same category as the word
preceding when a wider meaning is possible;
(iv) references to a document shall include that document as supplemented, amended,
replaced or updated from time to time;
(v) headings or titles are for convenience of reference only and not intended to interpret or
construe the meaning of this Agreement;
(vi) any reference to Articles and Annexes are references to the Articles and Annexes of this
Agreement; and
(vii) references to a person or a third party shall be construed as references to an individual
or a legal entity established under any form together with their successors or permitted
assigns.
