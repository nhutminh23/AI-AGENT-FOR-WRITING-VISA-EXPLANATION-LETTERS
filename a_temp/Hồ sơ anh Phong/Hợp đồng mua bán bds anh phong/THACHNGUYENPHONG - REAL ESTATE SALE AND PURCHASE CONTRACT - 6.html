Article 1. Golf course benefit package

1.1. Conditions for applying the Benefit Package:
The Seller commits that the Buyer shall enjoy benefits equivalent to the benefits of a PGA Garden golf club member under the Ocean Valley Tourism Complex Project for a single term of 35 years ("Benefit Package") when the Buyer fully meets the following conditions:

(a)Completing the signing of the Contract and/or other documents related to the Resort Real Estate as required by the Seller and having paid in full and on time the Phase 1 Sale Price of the Resort Real Estate as stipulated in the Contract;
(b)Fully complying with the Seller's regulations, instructions and/or the club's rules, regulations and policies during the use of the Benefit Package.

1.2. Content of the Benefit Package:

(a)The Buyer shall enjoy regimes and rights equivalent to the benefits granted to a 35 (thirty-five) year membership of the Club.
(b)For clarification, (i) the Buyer must still pay all actual costs incurred during golf play and/or use of other services at the Club, other service usage fees as prescribed by the Club at each time; and (ii) this Benefit Package shall be maintained until the end of 2025, from 2026 onwards, the Buyer shall pay the fees prescribed by the Club to maintain the Benefit Package rights.
(c)The Benefit Package shall be valid for use as notified by the Seller and/or the Club.
(d)The Parties agree that the content of the Benefit Package may change in accordance with the Club's policies, legal regulations and/or other objective reasons. In the event of such changes, the Seller commits that the Buyer shall still enjoy rights of equivalent value to the committed Benefit Package above. In such case, the Seller shall notify the Buyer at least 30 (thirty) days prior to the effective date of such changes (if any).
(e)The Buyer commits to fully perform financial obligations arising in relation to the Benefit Package in accordance with the law (if any).
(f)The Buyer has the right to transfer the rights under the Benefit Package to another person in accordance with the procedures and regulations of the Club. The transfer must be based on the pricing framework and transfer costs prescribed by the Club. For clarification, the Buyer may transfer only when (i) all rights and obligations under the Contract are concurrently transferred with the transfer of the Benefit Package rights; (ii) the Seller and/or the Club has notified that the Seller has consented to the use of the Benefit Package; (iii) the Buyer has paid in full at least 50% of the Sale Price of the Resort Real Estate; (iv) fully performed financial obligations as required by law when performing the transfer, and shall be responsible for paying taxes, fees and charges arising in relation to such transfer. The transferee shall enjoy the Benefit Package rights for the remaining corresponding term.
The Buyer commits that the transferee shall have no other claims and/or complaints, claims, disputes against the Seller related to the Benefit Package under this Appendix. Any disputes, claims, complaints (if any) shall be resolved between the Buyer and the transferee.

1.3. In case the Buyer violates any provision of the Contract or the Buyer terminates the Contract prior to the maturity, the Seller shall reclaim the Benefit Package and the Buyer shall pay the Seller the value of the Benefit Package corresponding to the time the Buyer has used from the time the Seller reclaims it. The Buyer is responsible for paying these costs as notified in writing by the Seller. The Buyer commits not to have any claims and/or complaints, claims, disputes against the Seller related to the Benefit Package that is reclaimed in accordance with this Article.
