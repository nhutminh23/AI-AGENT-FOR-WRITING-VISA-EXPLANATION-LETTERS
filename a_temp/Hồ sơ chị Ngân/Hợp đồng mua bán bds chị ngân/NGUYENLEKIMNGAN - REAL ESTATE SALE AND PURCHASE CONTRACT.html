ARTICLE 1: DEFINITION OF TERMS

1.1. “Resort Real Estate” means resort villas, shophouses within the auxiliary service area and other resort real estates of the Project constructed by the Investor for sale/long-term lease, details as provided in Article 2 of this Agreement.

1.2. “Handover Minutes” means the minutes prepared on the Handover Date for the purpose of handing over the Resort Real Estate. The Handover Minutes shall record the Actual Total Usable Floor Area and the Actual Site Area for the calculation of the Sale Price of the Resort Real Estate and other necessary related contents.

1.3. “Site Area” shall have the meaning as stipulated in Appendix 2.

1.4. “Project” means the real estate project of the Investor named NovaWorld Ho Tram – The Tropicana located at Ven Bien Street, Hamlet Binh Hai, Binh Chau Commune, Xuyen Moc District, Ba Ria - Vung Tau Province.

1.5. “Sale Price of the Resort Real Estate” means the total sale price of each Resort Real Estate as stipulated in Article 3 and Appendix 5 of this Agreement.

1.6. “Certificate” means the Certificate of land use right, ownership of house and other assets attached to land for the Resort Real Estate issued by the competent state authority to the Buyer in accordance with the law.

1.7. “Agreement” means this Resort Real Estate Sale and Purchase Agreement and all appendices annexed hereto as well as any amendments and supplements in writing to this Agreement made and signed by the Parties during the performance of this Agreement.

1.8. “Resort Real Estate Area” means the area comprising the Resort Real Estates of the Project.

1.9. “Common Area” means the common areas of the Resort Real Estate Area and the common areas of the Project as stipulated in Appendix 4 of this Agreement which the Buyer/occupants of the Resort Real Estate and the Seller shall use jointly in accordance with this Agreement.

1.10. “Private Area” means the area of the Project that is privately owned by the Buyer under this Agreement and/or privately owned by the Seller, as the context may require.

1.11. “Handover Date” means the date on which the Resort Real Estate is actually handed over to the Buyer as stipulated in Article 8.1 of this Agreement.

1.12. “Working Day” means a calendar day excluding Saturdays, Sundays, public holidays and Tet holidays as prescribed by Vietnamese law.

1.13. “Resort Real Estate Regulations” means all rules and regulations which the Buyer and/or occupants in the Resort Real Estate Area must comply with and observe in relation to the use of the Resort Real Estate, Common Areas, utility works during the management and use of the Resort Real Estate issued by the Seller, the management and operation unit of the Resort Real Estate Area or any party designated/authorized by the Seller.

1.14. “Force Majeure Event” means an event that occurs objectively which any Party could not foresee and could not overcome despite having applied all necessary measures and permitted capabilities. Cases regarded as Force Majeure Events are stipulated in Article 14 of this Agreement.

1.15. “Total Usable Floor Area” shall have the meaning as stipulated in Appendix 2.

(a) Words in the plural shall include the singular and vice versa;

ARTICLE 2: SALE AND PURCHASE OF RESORT REAL ESTATE

2.1. The Seller agrees to sell and the Buyer agrees to buy the Resort Real Estate for which the Seller is the Investor, with details and characteristics as set out in Appendix 2 of this Agreement.

2.2. In accordance with legal regulations, the Parties agree to determine the purposes of use of the Resort Real Estate as follows:

(a) The Buyer purchases the Resort Real Estate to use in accordance with (i) the purposes of use approved by the competent state authority, (ii) legal provisions and (iii) the manner of use mutually agreed by the Parties and stipulated in this Agreement and/or other agreements between the Parties.

(b) The Buyer shall not use the Resort Real Estate for any other purposes contrary to the purposes stipulated in Article 2.2(a) above.

ARTICLE 3: SALE PRICE OF THE RESORT REAL ESTATE, PAYMENT METHOD, PAYMENT TERM

3.1. The Sale Price of the Resort Real Estate is detailed in Appendix 5 of this Agreement.

3.2. Payment method:

(a) Payments under this Agreement shall be made in Vietnamese Dong, in the form of cash payment at the cashier office at the Seller’s official transaction locations or bank transfer into the Seller’s account as the account information stated at the beginning of this Agreement or another account as notified in writing by the Seller and credited to the Seller’s bank account with transfer fees and similar fees borne by the Buyer. In case the Buyer is a domestic organization, payment must be made by bank transfer.

(b) The Buyer shall be deemed to have fulfilled its payment obligations when the Seller issues a receipt for the payment received in full or the Seller’s bank account is credited with the Buyer’s payment. For clarity, in all cases, issuance and handing over of a value added tax invoice to the Buyer does not mean that the Buyer has fulfilled its payment obligations to the Seller.

(c) Payment shall only be considered valid when the Buyer complies with the above provisions. Payment in cash or by transfer to any other organization/person (including payment in cash or transfer to the Seller’s employee to pay on behalf) shall not be considered valid and the Seller shall not bear any responsibility in relation to such payments.

(d) The principle of determining the time when the Buyer completes its payment obligation shall apply even when this Agreement is assigned, transferred or terminated for any reason and the Buyer still has ongoing payment obligations arising from such assignment, transfer or termination.

(e) In case additional information or support is required relating to payment, the Buyer may contact the Seller via the Customer Care hotline or registered mail; or

(c) On the date of email dispatch immediately after successful sending in case of email. In this case, if the date of receipt is not a Working Day, then such notices, requests, claims or transactional letters shall be deemed received by the other Party on the next Working Day thereafter.

ARTICLE 17: DISPUTE RESOLUTION

In case of any dispute regarding and/or relating to the signing and/or performance of this Agreement, the Parties shall first resolve such dispute through negotiation within 30 (thirty) days from the date one Party notifies the other Party of the dispute. If this period expires and the dispute is not resolved by negotiation, then either Party shall have the right to request a competent People’s Court to resolve the case in accordance with the law.

All appendices to this Agreement shall amend, supplement and form an integral part of this Agreement.

18.2. In the event that the Parties agree to amend any contents of this Agreement and its appendices, such amendments must be made in writing with the seal (if the Party is a legal entity) and valid signature of the Parties.

18.3. This Agreement shall come into effect from the date it is validly signed by the Parties, and shall expire when the Parties have fulfilled their obligations under this Agreement, unless otherwise agreed.

18.4. This Agreement shall be construed, interpreted and governed by the laws of Vietnam.

18.5. This Agreement is made in 03 (three) Vietnamese-language counterparts of equal legal validity, the Buyer keeps 01 (one) counterpart, the Seller keeps 02 (two) counterparts for implementation.

2. The Sale Price of the Resort Real Estate does not include the following:

(a) Maintenance fee (if any); registration fee, fees and other costs (if any) in accordance with the law related to the implementation of procedures for issuance of the Certificate for the Buyer. These fees and costs shall be borne by the Buyer;

(b) Other financial obligations related to the Resort Real Estate as prescribed by law; all types of fees, costs reasonably required by the Seller including but not limited to repair costs of Project items; service fees and other costs arising from the provision of utilities. For clarity, the Buyer shall be responsible for these arising costs and expenses from the Handover Date, unless otherwise agreed by the Parties.

(c) Financial obligations related to land use rights in case land law provisions change causing the emergence of financial and land obligations (if any). For clarity, the Buyer shall be responsible to pay such arising financial obligations as notified by the Seller.

3. The Sale Price of the Resort Real Estate may be adjusted if at least one of the following cases occurs:

(a) The Parties agree that the Total Usable Floor Area may differ (increase or decrease) based on actual measurement on the Handover Date. If the actual Total Usable Floor Area differs within 2% (two percent) compared to the Total Usable Floor Area stated in Appendix 2 of this Agreement, the Parties shall not adjust the Sale Price of the Resort Real Estate. If the actual Total Usable Floor Area differs by more than 2% (two percent) compared to the Total Usable Floor Area specified in Appendix 2 of this Agreement, then the Sale Price of the Resort Real Estate excluding VAT shall be adjusted upward (+) or downward (-) correspondingly by the difference in Total Usable Floor Area multiplied (x) by 15,172,156 VND/m2.

(b) The Parties agree that the Site Area may differ (increase or decrease) based on actual measurement on the Handover Date. If the actual Site Area differs within 2% (two percent) compared to the Site Area specified in Appendix 2 of this Agreement, the Parties shall not adjust the Sale Price of the Resort Real Estate. If the actual Site Area differs by more than 2% (two percent) compared to the Site Area specified in Appendix 2 of this Agreement, then the Sale Price of the Resort Real Estate excluding VAT shall be adjusted upward (+) or downward (-) correspondingly by the difference in Site Area multiplied (x) by 726,695 VND/m2.

(c) In case VAT changes due to changes in VAT rate as prescribed by law during the performance of this Agreement, the Parties shall sign an appendix to adjust the Sale Price of the Resort Real Estate accordingly.

4. For avoidance of doubt, for the purposes of Clauses 3(a) and 3(b) above, the difference in Total Usable Floor Area and the difference in Site Area shall mean the actual area exceeding 2% (two percent) compared to the Total Usable Floor Area or Site Area. When comparing the Total Usable Floor Area and the Site Area, the Parties shall use the same measurement method. The actual Total Usable Floor Area and actual Site Area are the areas agreed by the Parties and recorded in the Handover Minutes.
